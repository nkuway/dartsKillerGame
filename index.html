Killers

Killers is a dart game where you need to kill the other player(s) by hitting your opponent(s) number(s).

Players

With Killers, the aim is to become a killer and kill you opponent(s) when you do.
You can become a killer by hitting your number and increase your points to five.
Your number is generated automatically when you fill in your name.
You can try to kill your opponent(s) by hitting the number(s) of the other player(s).
One hit equals one point and the player is dead when the points equal zero.
